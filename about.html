How It Started

A Kitchen Full of Dreams

Elegance Bakes was born in 2022 from a simple love of baking and a desire to make every celebration feel truly special. What started as weekend baking for family and friends quickly grew into something much bigger — a small-batch home bakery trusted by hundreds of families across Mumbai.

Every cake we make is baked fresh to order, with real ingredients and no preservatives. We don't do mass production — each creation gets the time, care, and creativity it deserves because we know your celebrations are once-in-a-lifetime moments.

We're proud to be FSSAI certified and operate our kitchen with commercial-grade hygiene standards, so you can order with complete confidence.
